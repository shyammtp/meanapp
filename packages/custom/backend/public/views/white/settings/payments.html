Offline Payments

Provide customers with instructions to pay outside of your online store. Choose from cash on delivery (COD), money order, bank deposit, or create a custom solution.

Bank deposit payment

If you enable more than one payment provider then your customers will need to choose which payment method they want to use. The text in this box will be used to describe this payment method on your site.

Which countries do you want to offer Bank Deposit to? If the customers billing country matches any of the selected countries then they will have the option to pay by Bank Deposit.

If a customer chooses to pay via bank deposit then he will be shown the text you type into this box once he completes his order.

You should include your bank details and other information here so the customer can pay for his order by bank deposit. The amount of the order will automatically be displayed.

Type in %%OrderID%% to insert the current Order ID

To integrate Bank Deposit into your store simply complete the form above and click the 'Save' button.
